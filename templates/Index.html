Organization Summary

Madres de Casino Road is a small BIPOC nonprofit organization that started during the summer of 2017. Initially, our goal was to empower women in their personal development through education. As we began to work with different groups within our community, we expanded our services to create safe environments for vulnerable families, serving Hispanic residents and other underserved groups within Snohomish County, Washington. Our mission continues to be focused on education because we believe that this is the best way to serve our community. Through our programs, we help to close the gap between people of diverse backgrounds, responding to racial, socio-economic, and gender divisions that divide our society. By providing services that address these inequities, we strengthen the social connections between our residents and give them the knowledge and resources they need to achieve their goals.

Vision

To be an active, agile and innovative community organization that responds proactively to the challenges we face and develops cutting-edge projects that help those who need it most; standing out above all for the dedication, ability and altruism of our members.

Mission

Our mission is to empower vulnerable Hispanic families in Snohomish County, giving them the support they need to achieve personal, educational, and professional success. Through these efforts, we strengthen our communities and strive to create a more equitable society.

Core Values

Commitment

To help our community progress.

Intimacy

To create an environment where each member of the family feels welcome.

Dedication

Help by providing the best of ourselves at the service of the community; welcoming vulnerable people regardless of their age, sex, race, origin, education or religion.

Development

Constant search to do more for our community; open to work with other organization, companies and foundations to achieve our aid objectives and the greatest benefit for the people we serve.

Integrity

We seek comprehensiveness in our accomplishment and training models, proposing durable solutions to eradicate the causes of exclusion and overcome personal, social or professional obstacles that make integration possible..

Honesty

In the management of our projects, for this we make our organization accessible and visible and we work to incorporate quality standards, evaluations and external activity controls.
